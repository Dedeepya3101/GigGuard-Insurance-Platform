## 💡 What is GigGuard?
GigGuard is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform that protects delivery workers from income loss caused by heavy rain, curfews, and other disruptions. It ensures workers receive compensation only when external conditions prevent them from working.

## 👤 Target Persona
**Food Delivery Partners (Zomato / Swiggy)**

Delivery workers depend on daily orders for income.  
When disruptions like rain or curfews occur, they lose earnings despite no fault of their own.  
GigGuard provides financial protection specifically for such situations.

## ⚙️ What it Does
- Protects income during disruptions  
- Uses AI to detect weather and external risks  
- Automatically triggers payouts  
- Eliminates manual claim process  

## 🔄 System Workflow
1. Delivery partner registers on the platform  
2. System collects location and activity data  
3. AI calculates weekly risk-based premium  
4. User subscribes to coverage  
5. System monitors weather and disruption data  
6. If disruption occurs → parametric trigger activates  
7. AI validates user location and activity  
8. Claim is automatically approved  
9. Instant payout is credited  

## 💰 Weekly Premium Model
GigGuard follows a **weekly subscription model** aligned with gig workers' earnings.

- Low-risk areas → ₹20/week  
- Medium-risk areas → ₹40/week  
- High-risk areas → ₹60/week  

Premium depends on:
- Rain frequency  
- Flood-prone zones  
- Heat conditions  
- Historical disruption data  

## ⚡ Parametric Triggers

### Environmental Triggers
- Rainfall > 15mm  
- Temperature > 45°C  
- Flood alerts  
- Pollution spikes  

### Social Triggers
- Curfews  
- Strikes  
- Lockdowns  

If triggered → payout is automatic
